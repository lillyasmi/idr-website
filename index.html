About IDR

Where academic rigour meets real-world risk.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

In partnership with a leading UK university, IDR bridges the gap between academic insight and operational practice. Our programmes don't just teach frameworks — they place trained practitioners into live projects, building the resilience that modern organisations urgently need.

Our Model

Three pillars. One mission.

From foundational bootcamps to enterprise advisory — IDR covers the full risk lifecycle for organisations and individuals alike.

01 / ACADEMY

Academy

Intensive bootcamps and structured learning pathways for students and working professionals seeking to enter or advance in digital and cyber risk roles.

Bootcamps Certifications CPD Apprenticeships
